2. Burger :

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Toppings of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Form beef into 4 patties.
  3. Season patties with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  5. Serve on hamburger buns with toppings.

4. Samosa :

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 lb potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. To make the dough, mix flour, salt, and oil. Add water and knead until smooth. Let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. To make the filling, heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  3. Add peas, potatoes, and spices. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Roll out dough into thin circles. Cut in half and shape into cones
